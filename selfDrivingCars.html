Self Driving Cars

What Are Autonomous Cars?

A car which can navigate, scan its surrounding and drive without any human input is called an autonomous car, also known as driverless, self-driving or robotic car.

In 2017 no cars had a permission to drive on public ro ads without a human behind the wheel who always has to be able to take control over the car.
